Questions people ask

If something's not covered here, you can ask the AI directly once you're in the app — or reach out via the contact info in our terms.

What exactly do I get for 299 kr?

A personalized 90-day plan built around your specific citizenship status, purpose for moving, and city — covering things like personnummer, BankID, bank account, and healthcare registration in the correct order. It also includes unlimited AI chat during your first 90 days to ask follow-up questions.

Is the information accurate?

We build the plan generator around official Migrationsverket and Skatteverket guidance and keep tightening it as we test more scenarios. That said, this is AI-generated general guidance, not legal advice — rules can change and every situation has its own details, so always confirm anything important on the official government sites before relying on it.

What languages does it support?

You can choose your preferred language when building your plan — including English, Svenska, العربية, Українська, हिन्दी, 中文, and dozens more. Official authority names (like Skatteverket or Migrationsverket) stay in Swedish since that's what you'll actually see on forms and letters.

Do I need to pay again for the Ongoing Support tier?

No — "Ongoing Support" is a separate optional add-on at 59 kr/month for people whose process runs longer than 90 days (multi-year visas, family reunification timelines). Most people only need the one-time plan.

Is my information stored anywhere?

No — your plan and chat history exist only in your browser during your session. We don't save your citizenship status, chat messages, or plan on our servers. See our privacy policy for details.

Can I get a refund?

If something genuinely went wrong on our end, yes — reach out within 14 days. Since this is general guidance rather than a guaranteed outcome, we can't refund simply because your personal situation turned out to need something different than the plan suggested.

Is Dag Ett affiliated with the Swedish government?

No. Dag Ett is an independent service, not affiliated with Migrationsverket, Skatteverket, Försäkringskassan, or any government agency.
