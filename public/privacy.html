Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Dag Ett ("we", "the service") helps people plan their first 90 days moving to Sweden. This page explains what information we collect, why, and what your rights are.

What we collect

When you use the plan generator, you provide:

  • Your citizenship status (e.g. EU/EEA citizen, Non-EU citizen)
  • Your purpose for moving (e.g. work, study, family reunification)
  • The city or region you're settling in
  • Your preferred language
  • Any questions you type into the chat feature

We do not ask for or collect your name, personnummer, passport details, immigration case number, or any uploaded documents.

How your information is used

Your inputs are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI to generate your personalized plan and answer chat questions. They are used only to produce your response — not to train AI models, and not shared with any other third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

Storage: as of this version, Dag Ett does not save your plan or chat history on our servers after your session ends. Your plan and chat only exist in your browser while you're using the site. If you download or copy your plan, that copy is yours to keep — we don't retain a server-side record of it.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not see or store your full card details — Stripe handles this directly under its own privacy policy and PCI-compliant systems. We receive confirmation that a payment was completed, along with a transaction reference.

Local storage

We use your browser's local storage to remember that you've paid, so you don't have to pay again if you reload the page. This stays on your device and isn't sent to us as tracking data.

Third parties we use

  • Anthropic — processes your plan/chat inputs to generate responses
  • Stripe — processes payments
  • Vercel — hosts the website and its backend functions

Your rights

Since we don't retain your personal data after your session, there is generally nothing stored for us to delete on request. If you have questions about how your data was handled during a session, or concerns about this service, you can reach out via the contact details below. Depending on your country, you may also have rights under GDPR or similar data protection law to request information, correction, or deletion of any data a service holds about you.

Changes to this policy

As Dag Ett develops, this policy may be updated — for example, if we add account creation or plan-saving features in the future, which would involve retaining more data. Any such change will be reflected here with an updated date.
